About

I've spent my career helping people find the right words.

As a journalist, I learned to tell stories that matter. In a decade of change management across Europe and China, I learned to translate complex ideas into language that moves people to action.

Now I help experts, business leaders, and coaches do the same—through books that capture their knowledge and establish their legacy.

I work with authors at every stage: from first idea to finished manuscript. Whether you want to write your own book with guidance, or hand over the writing entirely, I meet you where you are.

My clients include authors published through Brian Tracy Publishing, entrepreneurs building their personal brands, and professionals ready to share what they've learned with the world.

Based in England. Working globally.
